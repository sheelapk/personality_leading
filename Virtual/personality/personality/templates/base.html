Interview Tips

Interviewing is a learned skill, and there are no second chances to make a great first impression. so improve your skills in these areas • Practice good nonverbal communication
• Dress for the job or company
• Be Authentic, Focused, Confident and Concise
• Ask Insightful Questions
• Make Good First Impressions
• Sell Yourself and then Close the Deal
